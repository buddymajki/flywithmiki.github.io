Individual training for students

My philosophy

Welcome to flywithmiki, where safety is my top priority! I am committed to providing the highest quality paragliding instruction in a safe and fun environment. I offer individual coaching to ensure that you receive a personalized attention and guidance. I am passionate about sharing my love of paragliding with others and I am dedicated to helping students achieve their goals. I believe in a customized and personalized approach to training, tailoring each lesson to the individual needs of my students. This approach allows me to help each student reach their full potential and become confident and skilled paraglider pilots. I understand that taking to the skies can be intimidating, but rest assured that I take safety very seriously. I use only the highest quality standards and adhere to strict safety protocols to ensure that every flight is as safe as possible. So whether you're a beginner looking to take your first flight, or an experienced pilot looking to improve your skills, training with me is the perfect place to learn and grow. 

Join me today and discover the amazing world of paragliding!
