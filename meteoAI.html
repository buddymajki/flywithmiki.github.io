How the AI scores. Each day gets a flying-chance % for the best of the launch sites (Zugerberg, Brunni/Engelberg, Büelen/Wolfenschiessen, Niederbauen/Emmetten). Per site each of the 5 categories deducts points (yellow −10, red −20); any red caps the day at 50% (two reds 30%), fog trims it, and two hard NO-GOs force 0%: clear föhn (Δp≥4 hPa plus a real valley southerly, or ≥30 km/h southerly) or clear rain 08-15h at that site. ≥75% GO, 50-74% CAUTION, <50% NO-FLY. Model gusts only count when the base wind supports them — an isolated gust spike warns but cannot alone turn a day red. The confidence badge shows how much the algorithm trusts its own inputs (model range, model agreement, lead time). Storms compare ICON-CH1 + ICON-D2 per site (blended fallback beyond their range). The MeteoSwiss text keywords can only push a category toward more caution, never less. Decision support only — YOU are the pilot, YOU decide. Based on the SHV strategy.
