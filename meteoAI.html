How the AI scores (v3). Each day starts at 100% at each of the 4 launch sites (Zugerberg, Brunni/Engelberg, Büelen/Wolfenschiessen, Niederbauen/Emmetten) and everything that threatens a beginner course deducts points: rain is weighted by how many of the 9 course hours (09–17h) it likely eats, thunderstorms by ICON-CH1/ICON-D2 model agreement, föhn, wind strength & direction, fronts and fog by severity — small stuff (a few clouds) costs nothing. Two situations force 0%: clear föhn and rain through most of the course day. The best site's score is the day's %. Uncertainty itself costs points — if the models disagree or the day is far ahead, the score drops and the confidence badge shows why. The MeteoSwiss text keywords can only push the score down, never up. ≥75% GO · 50–74% CAUTION · <50% NO-FLY. The thermal forecast is a simplified parcel model on ICON data — a first estimate, not a sounding analysis. The algorithm is work in progress. Decision support only — YOU are the pilot, YOU decide.
