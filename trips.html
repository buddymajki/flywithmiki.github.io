TRIPS

Welcome to our "Trips" section! As a small, dedicated paragliding school, we are thrilled to offer specialized paragliding trips designed to cater to both our students and licensed pilots. Whether you are looking to refine your skills on the ground or soar to new heights across country, we have something exciting in store for you.

Advanced Ground Handling Camps

For our students, we organize Advanced Ground Handling Camps that focus on enhancing your control and confidence with your glider. These camps are designed to provide intensive, hands-on training in various wind conditions, helping you master the techniques needed for safe and effective glider handling. With personalized instruction from our experienced trainers, you will develop the skills necessary to smoothly launch and land your paraglider, gain confidence in the air and preparing you for more advanced flying experiences.

XC-Coaching for Licensed Pilots

For our licensed pilots seeking to push their boundaries, we offer Cross Country (XC) Coaching. These trips are tailored to help you improve your long-distance flying abilities, navigate complex airspaces, and understand advanced weather patterns. Our XC-Coaching sessions provide in-depth knowledge and practical experience, guiding you through the intricacies of route planning, thermal management, and efficient flying techniques. With our expert guidance, you can achieve your cross-country flying goals and gain the confidence to tackle more challenging flights.



Stay tuned for more details about these exciting trips. We are committed to providing exceptional training and memorable experiences that will take your paragliding skills to the next level. Whether you're perfecting your ground handling or aiming for ambitious cross-country flights, our specialized trips are designed to support your growth and enjoyment in the sport of paragliding.
