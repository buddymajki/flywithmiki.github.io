Paragliding Academy

Master your skills in a controlled environment

About the concept:
FLYwithMIKI's Paragliding Academy is a structured training program designed for licensed pilots who want to refine their skills, gain confidence, push their flying to the next level, or just simply enjoying organized events where they can fly together with another pilots under the supervision of an instructor.

Our academy provides expert coaching in key areas such as thermalling, cross-country flying, advanced ground handling, safety maneuvers, and hike & fly techniques. Through a combination of practical training and in-depth theory sessions, pilots enhance their understanding of flying conditions, maneuvers, decision-making, and risk assessment.

Members benefit from a supportive flying community, exclusive training days, and continuous guidance from experienced instructors. Whether you’re aiming to master XC flights, improve your landings, or take on new challenges in the air, this program offers structured, hands-on training tailored to your progress.

As an added benefit, academy members receive exclusive discounts on the best paragliding equipments , with savings of up to 30% over time. We don’t just sell gear, we personally test and recommend only the best and most up-to-date equipment, ensuring you have access to reliable, top-tier gear, which fits to your experience level and future goals.
