School - Fly High

Ground Handling - ​Mastering Skills on Solid Ground

Ground handling forms the bedrock of successful paragliding. It's where we learn to anticipate and react, to control the wing with precision. I emphasize the significance of mastering ground handling as it enables you to simulate various in-flight scenarios, preparing you for diverse airborne situations. Almost everything that could happen in the air can be simulated and practiced on the ground, enhancing your confidence and responsiveness as a pilot.

High Altitude Flights - ​Elevating Your Skills

Training at higher altitudes is pivotal in honing your maneuvers and preparing for examinations. My altitude flight training sessions are designed to familiarize you with high-altitude conditions, ensuring you become a safe and competent pilot. These sessions serve as an invaluable preparation for your exams and equip you with the necessary skills to navigate challenging terrains.

Theory Lectures - Understanding Every Aspect

My comprehensive theory lectures cover not just exam preparation but a deeper understanding of paragliding equipment and principles. It's crucial to comprehend the theoretical aspects to make informed decisions while in the air. These sessions empower you to grasp the mechanics behind paragliding, fostering confident and knowledgeable decision-making skills.

Mind Training - Overcoming Challenges

Paragliding isn't just about technical skills; it's about mental fortitude. Whether you're overcoming fear from a specific maneuver or restarting after an incident, I offer mind training sessions. Together, we work on cultivating a mindset that acknowledges your limits while consistently improving your skills. These sessions focus on building confidence, resilience, and a safety-oriented approach to flying.
