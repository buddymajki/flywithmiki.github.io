WHY CHOOSE US? Your flight is a personal journey

When choosing a paragliding school, it’s vital to recognize that flight is a truly personal journey. It’s an experience where you are constantly confronting and pushing past your own internal limits, as the air is fundamentally not our natural environment. This makes the role of your instructor paramount. At FLYwithMIKI, we commit to providing an instructor who will genuinely "hold your hand" throughout this process, offering unwavering support for your technical skills and, crucially, your mental development. This is why we insist on small group training—it’s the only way to ensure the highly personalized instruction necessary to focus intensely on each individual's progress, challenges, and confidence building.

We integrate this highly tailored approach with the very best in modern pedagogical methods and cutting-edge technology. Our training goes far beyond standard instruction by incorporating dedicated video analysis of your flights, focused one-on-one de-briefing sessions, and essential mental training. These tools are designed to accelerate your learning curve, improve your decision-making processes, and cement the confidence required for independent flight. Through this unique combination of personalized attention and advanced methods, we ensure you gain not just flying skills, but the profound psychological strength needed to excel as a pilot.

Our students receive free access to the unique Flight Deck app, an invaluable technological tool. This app allows students to track their own progress online, access all necessary theory and theoretical background in one place, and review their next steps even while on the mountain. This ensures that the learning process is continuous, efficient, and fully transparent.

Our ultimate goal is not just a certification, but transforming you into a safe, autonomous pilot who can make sound judgments and launch, fly, and land with unique confidence. Additionally, as the first paragliding school in Switzerland to fully embrace sustainability, we utilize an electric van and plant trees after every flight, ensuring your journey is also an environmentally responsible one.

Sustainability

Taking flight to new heights, one tree at a time, with every student's soar

I believe that caring for the environment is not just an option but a responsibility. With my background in engineering, I have always been passionate about developing eco-friendly technologies. Today, as a flying instructor and tandem pilot, I continue to prioritize the well-being of our planet.

Eco-Friendly Transportation

Committed to sustainability, my paragliding school uses an electric van and charges it with renewable energy whenever possible, thus minimizing our environmental footprint and setting a standard for eco-friendly adventure sports enthusiasts and responsible tourism practices.

Planting trees while soaring the skies

Plant Trees, Take Flight! We offset your paragliding adventure's carbon footprint. Our partner is planting Mangroves in Kenya (verified project), while we are flying in Switzerland. Up to four trees after each tandem flights and up to twentyfive trees planted after each student!
