When it comes to choosing a paragliding school, FLYwithMIKI.COM stands out for its commitment to personalized and intimate training experiences. Unlike larger schools, I believe in small group trainings that enable us to focus on each individual's needs. By keeping the group size limited, I can dedicate more time to discussing your progress, honing your skills, and addressing any concerns you may have. Moreover, our small groups allow us to fly alongside you, providing real-time guidance and the opportunity to practice maneuvers together in the air.

Additionally, FLYwithMIKI.COM takes pride in being at the forefront of sustainability practices in Switzerland. As the first paragliding school in the country to embrace sustainability, I utilize an electric van for transportation, reducing our carbon footprint and contributing to a greener environment. By choosing FLYwithMIKI.COM, you not only receive top-notch training but also support a school that prioritizes individual attention and environmental responsibility.

Sustainability

Taking flight to new heights, one tree at a time, with every student's soar

I believe that caring for the environment is not just an option but a responsibility. With my background in engineering, I have always been passionate about developing eco-friendly technologies. Today, as a flying instructor and tandem pilot, I continue to prioritize the well-being of our planet.

Eco-Friendly Transportation

Committed to sustainability, my paragliding school uses an electric van and charges it with renewable energy whenever possible, thus minimizing our environmental footprint and setting a standard for eco-friendly adventure sports enthusiasts and responsible tourism practices.

Planting trees while soaring the skies

Plant Trees, Take Flight! We offset your paragliding adventure's carbon footprint. Our partner is planting Mangroves in Kenya (verified project), while we are flying in Switzerland. Up to four trees after each tandem flights and up to twentyfive trees planted after each student!

Our tree planting initiative is verified by a reputable US-based tech company (Ecodrive-Veritree), ensuring transparency and genuine environmental impact, not green-washing. Click on the picture and check our verified tree planting project page on our partner's website
