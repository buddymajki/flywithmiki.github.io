Hello, I am
Miki

Miklos Mohos

I am an SHV certified paraglider instructor and cross country pilot, flying since 2016.
My passion is teaching paragliding and show the beauty of flying, in a safe way to students and already licensed pilots, independently from their piloting skills and experience.

I'm teaching in one of the biggest paragliding school in switzerland Paraworld.ch and I offer a more personalized individual training through flywithmiki.com.

I use a modern, individualized instruction method, so during the one to one coaching, you can progress on your self-paced learning curve towards your goals.
