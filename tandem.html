How to prepare?

Dress Comfortably: Wear comfortable clothes that allow for ease of movement. Regardless of the season, bring along a light jacket or sweater. Weather conditions can change at higher altitudes. Even during the warmer months, having an extra layer can make your experience more enjoyable. 
Footwear Matters: Closed-toe shoes or boots are essential for your safety. Choose footwear that provides good ankle protection. 
Eye Protection: Feel free to wear your favorite shades as we glide through the air.
Personal Belongings: You're welcome to bring small personal items such as your cell phone, wallet, and water bottle. These can be safely secured in the harness during the flight, so they don't go flying during the flight.
