When it comes to choosing a paragliding school, FLYwithMIKI.COM stands out for its commitment to personalized and intimate training experiences. Small group approach

Learning paragliding in a small group environment is of paramount importance for a comprehensive and personalized learning experience. My approach prioritizes quality over quantity. 
By limiting the group size, I ensure that each individual receives undivided attention and tailored guidance throughout the training journey, both in the air and on the ground.

This focused attention allows for quicker skill acquisition, better comprehension of techniques, and heightened safety awareness. Unlike the lengthy waiting times and diluted instruction found in crowded classes, my small group trainings guarantee an immersive and hands-on educational atmosphere. With a maximum of five participants per instructor on the training hill and eight during high flights, students can thrive in a supportive community while building confidence and proficiency in paragliding.

Learn to fly, from the first steps on the ground, up to the cloudbase with eagles at FLYwithMIKI.COM! 

Sustainability

I believe that caring for the environment is not just an option but a responsibility. 
With my background in engineering, I have always been passionate about developing eco-friendly technologies. Today, as a flying instructor and tandem pilot, I continue to prioritize the well-being of our planet. 
My paragliding school is committed to minimizing our carbon footprint in every aspect of our operations. I am proud to be the first paragliding school in Switzerland to utilize a fully electric van for transportation. 

My dedication to sustainability goes beyond just using an electric van. I recognize the significance of the energy source itself. That's why I make it a priority to charge the van's batteries with 100% renewable energy whenever possible. By relying on clean and sustainable power sources, I am actively working to make a positive impact on the environment and reduce our reliance on fossil fuels.

Paragliding meets sustainability

At ​FLYwithMIKI.COM, I believe that enjoying the exhilaration of paragliding should not come at the expense of our planet. Through my sustainable practices, I hope to inspire others to embrace eco-friendly alternatives and take part in preserving the beauty of our natural surroundings.
Join me at flywithmiki.com, where you can soar through the skies while knowing that you're doing your part for a greener future.

Your journey:

Training hill: ​Our initial days will be spent on the training hill, providing you with a solid foundation in equipment familiarity and pre-flight preparations. You'll engage in low-altitude flights, focusing on safe takeoffs and landings, all while ensuring an enjoyable learning experience.
High flights: ​Once you've confidently mastered takeoffs and landings, our focus shifts to honing your skills in the air. During the flights where you will have several hundred meters between your harness and the ground, we'll refine your techniques and introduce maneuvers that contribute to your growth as a proficient and safe pilot.
Advanced ground handling: ​Elevating your proficiency significantly, I emphasize the importance of mastering ground handling. This fundamental aspect of your flying journey is best cultivated through practice and refinement, and we continually revisit this essential skill set.

XC-flights: Upon earning your license, your flying journey truly unfolds. Cross-country flights open up new horizons, and my guidance remains available for your flights under my supervision. Your growth as a pilot is nurtured through these progressive experiences, ensuring both skill enhancement and safety.
