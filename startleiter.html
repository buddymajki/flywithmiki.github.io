Takeoff Marshall (Startleiter) Program

Role Overview

  • Welcome students and guide them through takeoff briefings
  • Help with glider setup and preparation at launch sites
  • Double-check equipment and harnesses for safety
  • Keep an eye on wind and weather conditions
  • Support clear, confident radio communication
  • Ensure smooth, safe launches every time

Minimum Requirements

  • Valid paragliding pilot license (SHV or IPPI 4 equivalent)
  • Required insurances and permits for freelance work in Switzerland
  • Completed FLYwithMIKI Startleiter training (online or in-person)
  • Comfortable communicating in English and German
  • Safety-focused, responsible, and a team player
