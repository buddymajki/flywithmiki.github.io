XC-Trip in Switzerland

Make your first 50-75-100 km flight

Make your first bigger "step" on the map :)

The concept

Embark on an exhilarating cross-country paragliding adventure in the breathtaking landscapes of Switzerland with our experienced guides, Marcel Schmid and Miki Mohos. This exclusive trip is designed for licensed pilots seeking to master the fundamentals of XC flying, surpass personal records, and achieve remarkable milestones. Join our small group, where every participant receives personalized attention and guidance to accomplish their first 30-50-75 or even 100 km flight. The journey promises not just a thrilling experience but also the opportunity to earn prestigious CIVL-FAI badges, including Bronze, Silver, or Gold, for those who meet the requirements.

EARN YOUR FAI BADGE

In case one of your flights fulfills the FAI/CIVL requirements during the trip, then you will get your FAI BRONZE/SILVER/GOLD badge on the last debriefing session.
