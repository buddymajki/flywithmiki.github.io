How this works. This page shows only objective weather data for today + 4 days — learn to read it yourself first, following the SHV 5-point check: general situation, fronts, föhn, nationwide wind, regional wind, storms, plus fog. Sources: MeteoSwiss official text, DWD front charts, profiwetter föhn/Bise/high-fog diagrams, XC-Therm wind map and Open-Meteo model data (all wind in km/h). When you have formed your own picture, open the AI prediction and compare. Decision support only — YOU are the pilot, YOU decide. Based on the SHV strategy.
