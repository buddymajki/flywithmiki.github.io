Sustainability

At flywithmiki.com, I believe that caring for the environment is not just an option but a responsibility. 
With my background in engineering, I have always been passionate about developing eco-friendly technologies. Today, as a flying instructor and tandem pilot, I continue to prioritize the well-being of our planet. 

My paragliding school is committed to minimizing our carbon footprint in every aspect of our operations. I am proud to be the first paragliding school in Switzerland to utilize a fully electric van for transportation. 

My dedication to sustainability goes beyond just using an electric van. I recognize the significance of the energy source itself. That's why I make it a priority to charge the van's batteries with 100% renewable energy whenever possible. By relying on clean and sustainable power sources, I am actively working to make a positive impact on the environment and reduce our reliance on fossil fuels.

Paragliding meets sustainability

At flywithmiki.com, I believe that enjoying the exhilaration of paragliding should not come at the expense of our planet. Through my sustainable practices, I hope to inspire others to embrace eco-friendly alternatives and take part in preserving the beauty of our natural surroundings.
Join me at flywithmiki.com, where you can soar through the skies while knowing that you're doing your part for a greener future.
