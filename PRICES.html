Courses for students

Full training: (until your IPPI4 - Swiss Pilot license)
1-2 Instructional tandem flight with Miki (optional)
Ground handling and training hill (3-5 days)
High altitude flights (up to 12 months from the 1st high flight)
Theory lections (Aerodynamics, Meteorology, Flying practice, Material Sciences, Air law) 
Mind training
Material included until the first 5 high flights

Basic training: 
1 Instructional tandem (optional)
Ground handling and training hill (3-5 days)
High Altitude flights on the last day (1-3 flights)
Material included during the entire course

School extension 
Additional 3 months and unlimited flights with the school

Guest schooling (1 day) 
1 day training in small group environment. Practice maneuvers, Prepare for your exam (Wolfenschiessen - Büelen). Requirement: minimum 30 high flights and own equipment.

Taster day
Try out paragliding on the training hill (4-6h)
In case you sign up for the FULL TRAINING in 5 days from your taster, you get a CHF 250 discount from your glider.

Courses for pilots

Visiting pilot (1 day)
for pilots with IPPI 1-4 or APPI license
Prepare for your Swiss exam (IPPI License naturalization) or fly in a safe environment in the Swiss alps as a visiting pilot under my supervision. Meteo and Airspace briefing, maneuver briefing, radio supervision.

Solo exam preparation
For instructor and tandem licenses
up to 20 high altitude flights under my guidance
maneuver briefing and training for the exam
XC hints: giving my advice how and where to make your mandatory 50km+ XC flight

Tandem 1
Instructional tandem flight
20 high altitude flights under my guidance
maneuver training for your tandem 1 exam

Tandem 2
30 high altitude flights under my guidance
maneuver training for your tandem 3 exam

Tandem FULL
Solo exam prep. + Tandem 1 + Tandem 3
The price is only valid if you buy your full tandem equipment from me (glider+harnesses+rescue)
