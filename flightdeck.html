✈ What is FlightDeck?

FlightDeck is our dedicated paragliding app for tracking your progress, logging flights, managing your training milestones, and staying connected with your instructor. It replaces the traditional paper logbook with a modern, always-with-you digital solution.

✅ Free for students

FlightDeck is completely free to use for all FLYwithMIKI students — no subscription, no hidden fees.

📊 Track your progress

Log every flight, monitor your training milestones, and review your flight history at any time.

📱 Always with you

Access your logbook from your smartphone, tablet, or computer — no paper required.

🔗 Connected to your school

Miki can see your progress and provide personalized guidance based on your actual flight data.
