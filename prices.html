Small group training, modern teaching methods

You're a beginner looking to take your first flight or an experienced pilot aiming to refine your skills, our courses are tailored to help you achieve your goals in a safe, supportive, and professional environment.

We offer a range of courses designed to suit all skill levels and needs. Our pricing is competitive, reflecting the high quality of instruction and the individualized attention you will receive. The total costs for obtaining a paragliding license in Switzerland comprise three main elements: education, materials, and miscellaneous expenses. Altogether, aspiring paragliders should budget between 9,000 to 10,000 CHF to cover all the essential costs associated with obtaining their paragliding license in Switzerland.


Our Basic training is conducted in small groups of 1-3 students per instructor. This almost individual training approach ensures the steepest learning curve, allowing you to progress quickly and confidently. We utilize modern teaching methods, including slow-motion video analysis and recursive feedback, to provide comprehensive and detailed instruction. Our commitment to these advanced techniques guarantees that you receive the best possible education in paragliding.


During high altitude flights, we fly in small groups to ensure you can make the most of your flying day. This setup allows our instructors to fully focus on your flights, giving you ample time to discuss maneuvers and techniques after each session. In some cases, your instructor will even fly alongside you, providing immediate feedback and guidance to enhance your skills in real-time.

Details of our main courses

Full Training(when full equipment purchased from us)

Become a certified paraglider pilot with our comprehensive training program that prepares you for the Swiss IPPI4 pilot license.

  • 🌱 Twenty-five planted trees
  • 1 Instructional tandem flights with Miki (optional)
  • Basic training (3-5 days)
  • Unlimited high altitude flights (up to 12 months from the 1st flight)
  • Theory lessons (Aerodynamics, Meteorology, Flying, Material Sciences, Air Law)
  • Material included (until the first 3 high flights)

Basic training

Learn the basics of paragliding in a safe and controlled environment. This course (3-5 days) is a great introduction to the sport.

  • 🌱 Ten planted trees
  • 1 Instructional tandem flight with Miki (optional)
  • Ground handling and training hill exercises
  • Low altitude flights
  • High Altitude flights on the last day (1-3 flights)
  • Material included during the entire course

High Altitude Flights

You already have your equipment and did the basics somewhere else? Does not matter, join our school and progress with us.

  • 🌱 Ten planted trees
  • 1 Instructional tandem flights with Miki (optional)
  • High altitude flights (up to 6 months or 60 flights, whichever comes first)
  • Theory lessons (Aerodynamics, Meteorology, Flying, Material Sciences, Air Law)

School extension

You already did your full training / high flights with us, but you want to extend your time in the school? No problem :).

  • 🌱 Four planted trees
  • Unlimited high altitude flights (up to 3 months)

Guest Student / Exam Simulation / Visiting pilot

Spend one day in our school to learn new flying sites, practice maneuvers or prepare for your exam

  • 🌱 One planted tree
  • Briefing according to your goals (Meteorology, Legislation, Maneuver, Exam)
  • High altitude flights (unlimited flights during the day (3-8))
