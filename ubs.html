About Our School

FLYwithMIKI is a small, eco-conscious paragliding school based in Kriens, Switzerland. Our goal is to offer personalized, high-quality paragliding training in small groups, with modern teaching methods in both English and German. We provide a complete experience – from shuttle service to expert guidance in stunning Alpine landscapes.

Why Paragliding?

Paragliding is not just a sport, it's an experience of freedom and connection with nature. Whether you're flying solo or enjoying a tandem flight, it’s a chance to soar over breathtaking scenery, explore new perspectives, and challenge yourself in ways that are both thrilling and fulfilling.
