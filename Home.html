Hello, I am
Miki

Miklos Mohos

SHV certified paraglider instructor and cross country pilot, flying since 2016.

My passion is teaching paragliding and show the beauty of flying, in a safe way to students and already licensed pilots, independently from their piloting skills and experience.

I'm teaching in one of the biggest paragliding school in switzerland (Paraworld.ch). In addition, I offer personalized individual training through flywithmiki.com. I use modern, personalized instruction methods, so during the one to one coaching, you can progress on your self-paced learning curve towards your goals.
