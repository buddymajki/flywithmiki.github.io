Tandem Flights

Take your flying experience to new heights! Whether you're a licensed pilot looking to hone your skills and maneuvers, or simply seeking the thrill of soaring through the sky, Miki has got you covered. 
With years of experience and a passion for paragliding, Miki will guide you through the skies, offering expert instruction and guidance every step of the way. 
You'll feel the wind in your hair and the sun on your face as you glide effortlessly through the air, taking in breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
Experience the thrill of thermalling, improve your flying skills, or simply enjoy the ride - the choice is yours!
Whatever your goals, Miki's paragliding tandem flights are the perfect way to take your love of flying to the next level.Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to experience the beauty and freedom of paragliding. 

Book your tandem flight with Miki today and discover the joy of flying like never before!
